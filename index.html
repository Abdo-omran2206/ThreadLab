🔍 Why ThreadLab?

The old way is broken.

Traditional design tools weren't built for apparel designers who need to see results in real time.

😤 The Problem
  • ✕ 2D design tools give you a flat preview — no way to see how it looks on a real garment.
  • ✕ Photoshop and Illustrator are heavy software — expensive and steep learning curves.
  • ✕ No real-time 3D preview — you don't know how the final product looks until it's printed.
  • ✕ Mockup generators are static — they can't show you the design from multiple angles.
  • ✕ Export quality is limited — most tools only give you one resolution option.
✅ ThreadLab Solves It
  • ✓ Real-time 3D rendering — see every design change reflected instantly on the 3D model.
  • ✓ 100% browser-based — open the link and start designing. Zero installation.
  • ✓ Multi-angle preview — rotate, zoom, and inspect from any angle before exporting.
  • ✓ Layer-based editor — stack colors, logos, and text just like a professional tool.
  • ✓ Pro exports — 4× Ultra-Res PNG, WebM video turntable, or raw GLB 3D model.
